Picshare - share with friends!

Ever been to a party and wanted to collect all the pictures you and your friends took in one place?

Picshare allows you to share and collaborate on albums with your friends. You and your friends can all upload, comment and edit the album. At any point, you can view the album online or download it (at the original resolution).

To start, simply drag some images onto the page.
